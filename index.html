Yamanouchi-machi Shimotakai-gun

SPA

It is believed that a baby monkey one day had the curiosity and the courage to venture in to the hot spring for the first time. This behavior then spread through the troop, and the Wild Monkey Park established hot spring baths exclusively for the monkeys. Japanese Macaque monkeys inhabit a more northerly area than any other type of monkey. Jigokudani wild monkeys bathe in the hot springs to endure the severe cold in the winter season, and it is uncommon to see them relaxing in the hot water in warmer seasons.
